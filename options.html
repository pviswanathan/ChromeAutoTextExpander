Auto Text Expander Feedback Feature Requests

Edit your database of shortcuts and expansions here! Useful tips.

  • Add or edit your shortcuts on the fly through the (txt) icon that shows up next to the url bar.
  • Shortcuts will auto-capitalize or auto-all-caps! Just capitalize the first letter in a multi-letter shortcut, or type the entire shortcut in caps to trigger all-caps.
  • Omnibox (url address bar) now supported! Type

                    Backing up your shortcuts with the "Backup" button only makes an 
                    extra copy of them in Chrome's local storage quota. This means if
                    Chrome's syncing gets messed up, you can restore from your backup,
                    but if you sign out of your browser or uninstall the extension, 
                    you will lose your backup. You can get a copy of your shortcuts from
                    the Import/Export dialog.

                    here.
